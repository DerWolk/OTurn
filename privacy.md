## English

### 1. Overview
OTurn is an offline app for fair task distribution in teams. Your privacy is important to us.

### 2. Data Collection
**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

### 3. Local Data Storage
- All data (group names, tasks, history) is stored exclusively locally on your device
- No transmission to external servers or cloud services
- Data never leaves your device

### 4. Camera Permission
- Used only for OCR text recognition
- Recognizes names from photos for automatic group creation
- No images are stored or transmitted
- Camera access can be disabled anytime in iOS Settings

### 5. Internet Connection
- OTurn works 100% offline
- No network connections required or used
